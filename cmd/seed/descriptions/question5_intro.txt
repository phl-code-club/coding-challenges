***Oh no!*** It seems the Mad Mage has put the city under a hex again! 

You are stuck in one of his winding mazes. You will need to find a way out so you can get to his tower to reverse the spell.

***Input format:***

- `rows`: A grid map where each cell contains either a path (`.`) or a wall (`#`).
- `queries`: Remaining lines in format `start_row start_col end_row end_col`

You can move up, down, left, right to adjacent path cells. Coordinates are 0-indexed.
