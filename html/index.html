Welcome to ⚔️Hunting for Gems 🧭

You will be paired with another person and given a list of problems that need solving! Each problem is split up into the following:

  • Name
  • Intro
  • Part 1 with example
  • Part 2 with example

Each question will give you an input. All you need to do is take that input and use it to derive an output. Sometimes these outputs are just numbers, sometimes they are a list of comma separated values.

To start head over here to create your team. Once you have a team you can go here to see a list of questions!
